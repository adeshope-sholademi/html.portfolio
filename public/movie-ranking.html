Favourite K-dramas of all time

My favourite series

Mr Queen

Genre: Comedy
Historical drama about a successful and free-spirited chef that finds himself in a strange situation when he wakes up in the body of Queen Cheorin in the Joseon period.

Vincenzo

Genre: Action
A series about a Korean-Italian mafia lawyer that gave a conglomerate a taste of its own medicine with a side of justice during a visit to the motherland

What's Wrong with Secretary Kim

Genre: Romance
Lee Young-joon is an egocentric but successful corporate executive. When Kim Mi-so, his secretary, decides to resign due to an unknown reason, he tries everything in his power to change her mind.
